HOW and WHO

How

Physical meet ups in Gothenburg, Sweden. You as part of the community choose what to learn. Check the boxes of what you want to learn and submit. When enough people have shown interest, the Community will arrange a learning session.

Who

Everyone who is friendly and open-minded is welcome. We strive for an inclusive environment and do not tolerate racism, homophobia, transphobia, or discrimination based on disability.


In addition to acquiring new knowledge, we aspire to forge new friendships with individuals we might not have crossed paths with otherwise.

Is there a cost?

At times, yes. Our goal is to keep activities accessible to everyone free of charge. However, in certain instances, we may need to enlist the expertise of a professional, which could incur a cost.


If you're unable to pay for any reason, please don't hesitate to inform us, and we'll find a solution. For those who can contribute a little extra, your generosity is greatly appreciated, as it helps ensure that as many people as possible can participate.
