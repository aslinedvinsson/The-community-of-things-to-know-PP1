who

Everybody who is friendly and openminded. We want an incluse environment and do not tolerate any racism, homofobi, transfobia, disability-fobia.


Besides learning new things we hope to make new friends, friends that we otherwise maybe wouldn’t have met.

Does it cost money?

Sometimes yes. The aim is for the activities to be free for all, but in some cases we need to pay a professional to teach us. For you who can’t pay for any reason, just let us know and we figure something out. For you who have a little bit of extra are very welcome to pay a higher fee, so as many people as possible can join.
